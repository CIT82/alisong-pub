Our Delicious Menu

Experience a taste of Japan right here in San Francisco! Our menu brings authentic Japanese flavors to the heart of the city, with every bowl of ramen crafted from rich, savory broths and fresh, handcrafted ingredients. From classic tonkotsu to flavorful miso, each dish transports you to the streets of Japan with every bite. Whether you're a ramen enthusiast or a first-timer, our menu is your gateway to a true Japanese dining experience—no passport required!
