Are your ramen dishes MSG free?

We believe in creating ramen that's both delicious and safe to enjoy. That's why we use MSG (monosodium glutamate) responsibly to enhance the natural flavors of our ingredients. MSG is a widely used and FDA-approved flavor enhancer, adding a rich, umami depth to dishes. Scientific studies have shown that, when used in moderation, MSG is safe for consumption and helps bring out the best in the food we serve. Rest assured, every bowl of ramen at Slurp Symphony is crafted with care and balanced flavor.

Are your noodles gluten free?

At Slurp Symphony, we want everyone to enjoy the perfect bowl of ramen. That's why we offer a choice between our traditional wheat noodles and gluten-free brown rice noodles. Whether you're gluten-sensitive or simply prefer a healthier alternative, our brown rice noodles provide a deliciously wholesome option without compromising on taste or texture. Just let us know your preference when you order!

How do you store leftover ramen?

To enjoy your leftover ramen at its best, we recommend storing the noodles and broth separately. Place the noodles in an airtight container and refrigerate the broth in a sealed container as well. This prevents the noodles from becoming too soft. When you're ready to eat, simply reheat the broth and pour it over the noodles for a fresh-tasting bowl of ramen. Leftovers should be consumed within 1-2 days for optimal flavor!
