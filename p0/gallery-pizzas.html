Our Pizza Pies

At Sage & Salt, the gourmet pizza is a culinary masterpiece, crafted with the finest ingredients to create a symphony of flavors. The dough is hand-stretched to perfection, offering a delicate balance between a crispy crust and a tender, airy interior. Each pizza is topped with organic, locally sourced produce and premium cheeses like creamy burrata or rich buffalo mozzarella. Artisanal meats, such as prosciutto di Parma and truffle-infused salami, enhance the flavors, while house-made sauces—ranging from classic San Marzano tomato to basil pesto—bring each bite to life. Finished with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and fresh herbs, every pizza is a refined expression of gourmet Italian tradition, designed to impress even the most discerning palate.
